Suburban densification dashboard

Science based and data-driven study of densification in suburban areas of 6 european cities. This portal is a proof of concept of a collaborative dashboard patchwork aiming at building comparable maps for the study of suburban densification across 6 european cities, at interpreting these maps to produce a deeper understanding of this phenomenon, its drivers and impacts. The collaboration is focused on : specifying the maps, producing them, interpreting them. Throughout the process, the management of possible biases and context specific consideration is key for the soudness of the comparative analysis.

QUESTIONS : trying to understand what is suburban densification, its drivers and impacts, more and more specific questions are needed to specify new maps. Questions such as : Where does densification happen? Why are some parcels left unused? How do people perceive densification ? etc.
