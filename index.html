Suburban densification dashboard

This dashboard patchwork is dedicated to the study of suburban densification across 6 european cities : Dortmund, Frankfurt, Strasbourg, Toulouse, Liverpool, Bristol Patchwork because we use different local data. It is currently under design during a collaborative projects.

Trying to understand suburban densification, its drivers and impacts, specific QUESTIONS are needed. Where does densification happen? Why are some parcels left unused? How do people perceive densification ? etc.

Science-based CONCEPTS allow us to connect questions with required maps that will participate to our dashboard patchwork.
